About the Event

Join us for an exciting one-day workshop designed for beginners and aspiring web developers. During this event, participants will gain practical knowledge of HTML5, CSS3, Git, GitHub, responsive web design, and modern web development practices.

Whether you are starting your programming journey or improving your existing skills, this workshop offers an excellent opportunity to learn through practical exercises and networking.
