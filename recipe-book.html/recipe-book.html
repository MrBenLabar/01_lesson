Jollof Rice

Jollof Rice is one of the most famous dishes in West Africa. It is a flavorful rice dish cooked with tomatoes, peppers, onions, and spices.

Recipe Details

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 45 minutes
  • Servings: 6 people
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Rice 4 cups
Tomatoes 6 medium
Onions 2 large
Vegetable Oil 1 cup
Seasoning Cubes 2 cubes

Cooking Steps

  1. Wash and parboil the rice.
  2. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onions.
  3. Fry the tomato mixture in hot oil.
  4. Add seasoning and stock.
  5. Add rice and cook until tender.
  6. Serve hot with chicken, fish, or salad.

Source: Wikipedia - Jollof Rice

Egusi Soup

Egusi Soup is a rich Nigerian soup prepared using ground melon seeds, vegetables, meat, and palm oil. It is commonly served with pounded yam, eba, or fufu.

Recipe Details

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 50 minutes
  • Servings: 8 people
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Ground Egusi 2 cups
Palm Oil 1 cup
Spinach 2 bunches
Beef 500g
Seasoning Cubes 2 cubes

Cooking Steps

  1. Cook meat with seasoning until tender.
  2. Heat palm oil in a pot.
  3. Add ground egusi and stir.
  4. Add stock and cooked meat.
  5. Add vegetables and simmer.
  6. Serve with pounded yam or eba.

Source: Wikipedia - Egusi Soup

Puff Puff

Puff Puff is a sweet fried dough snack enjoyed across Nigeria. It is soft, fluffy, and perfect for parties or casual treats.

Recipe Details

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 20 pieces
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 4 cups
Sugar 1/2 cup
Yeast 2 teaspoons
Warm Water 3 cups

Cooking Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and yeast.
  2. Add warm water and stir thoroughly.
  3. Allow the batter to rise for 45 minutes.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan.
  5. Drop batter into the oil.
  6. Fry until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm.

Source: Wikipedia - Puff Puff
