Why Visit Mambilla Plateau?

Located in the southeastern corner of Taraba State, Mambilla Plateau is Nigeria's highest plateau. Famous for its breathtaking scenery, rolling hills, cool climate, waterfalls, tea plantations, and rich cultural heritage, it offers a unique travel experience unlike anywhere else in Nigeria.

Visitors can enjoy panoramic mountain views, peaceful countryside landscapes, wildlife, and outdoor adventures while experiencing the hospitality of the local communities.

Top Attractions

  • Chappal Waddi (Nigeria's Highest Mountain)
  • Mambilla Tea Plantation
  • Gashaka-Gumti National Park
  • Beautiful Waterfalls
  • Rolling Green Hills and Valleys

Things To Do

  1. Hike to Chappal Waddi
  2. Explore tea plantations
  3. Visit waterfalls and rivers
  4. Go bird watching
  5. Take nature photography tours
  6. Experience local culture and cuisine

Travel Information

The best time to visit Mambilla Plateau is during the dry season between November and March. The plateau enjoys a cool climate all year round, making it an excellent destination for relaxation and adventure.
